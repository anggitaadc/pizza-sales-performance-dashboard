# 🍕 Pizza Sales Dashboard (Power BI)

## 📌 Project Overview
This project showcases an interactive sales dashboard built using Microsoft Power BI to analyze pizza sales performance. The dashboard transforms raw transactional data into meaningful insights to support business decision-making and performance monitoring.

---

## 🎯 Business Objective
- Analyze overall sales performance and profitability  
- Identify top-performing pizza categories and sizes  
- Understand revenue distribution and pricing patterns  
- Monitor sales trends over time  
- Support strategic decisions to improve revenue and profit  

---

## 📊 Key Metrics
- **Total Pizza Types**: 32  
- **Total Quantity Sold**: 1.02M  
- **Total Revenue**: 817.86K  
- **Net Revenue**: 858.75K  
- **Total Profit**: 40.89K  

---

## 📊 Key Features
- **Sales Trend Analysis**: Daily profit trends across the year to identify fluctuations and seasonality  
- **Category Performance**: Revenue comparison across pizza categories (Classic, Supreme, Chicken, Veggie)  
- **Pricing Analysis**: Average unit price by pizza category  
- **Size Distribution**: Contribution of each pizza size (S, M, L, XL, XXL) to total sales  
- **Profitability Analysis**: Profit, tax, and gross profit margin breakdown  
- **Interactive Filtering**: Dynamic filters by pizza name, category, and ingredients  

---

## 📈 Key Insights
- **Classic pizzas** generate the highest revenue among all categories  
- **Large (L) size pizzas** contribute the largest share of total sales volume  
- Profit margins are relatively stable (~4.8%), indicating consistent pricing strategy  
- Daily profit trends show fluctuations but remain within a stable range  
- Premium categories (Chicken & Supreme) have higher average prices
